T1D Overview

Management Framework

  1. Project Management: Progress tracking, Resource optimization
  2. Risk Management: Risk identification, Seller engagement strategy
  3. Knowledge Management: Knowledge accumulation, Sharing, Application

Tactical Implementation

  1. Precisely selecting suitable sellers
  2. Targeted management and cultivation system
  3. Phase outcomes and future plans

Goal

Increase the number of T3Y CN Global Selling Sellers in Established marketplaces reaching $250K TTM GMS from 49.3K to 49.6K, targeting a 0.73% YoY growth.

Seller Tier Framework

Tiering Logic: Identified based on TTM GMS, FBA BA performance and GMS growth potential.

T1 Retain

TTM GMS: >250K

Sellers with proven high GMS achievement or strong growth momentum, showcasing operational excellence and program maturity while maintaining self-sufficiency with minimal AM support

T1D High-Potential

TTM GMS: 180K-250K

Sellers demonstrating high growth potential but requiring AM guidance to fully realize their GMS targets

T1D Mass

TTM GMS: 50K-180K (Y2) and 100K-180K (Y3)

Early-stage sellers with moderate GMS performance and growth indicators.

Seller Selection Mentor Model

T1 Retain

Full monitoring on 200-400K sellers since they have highest tier-down risk. Focus on the bottom 30% performers in GMS growth rate.

T1D High-Po

Full monitoring on 180-250K sellers.

T1D Mass

Target top 10%/ 30% performers in GMS growth rate for 50-180K sellers.

Seller Distribution

Calculation Logic

  1. T3Y GMS mix = GMS for T1D managed Y2,Y3 seller in 50-500K / GMS for Trailing 3 Years sellers in 50-500K
  2. Total GMS mix = GMS for T1D managed Y2,Y3 seller in 50-500K / GMS for all tenure sellers
  3. T3Y seller mix = T1D managed Y2,Y3 seller in 50-500K / Trailing 3 Years sellers in 50-500K
  4. Total seller mix = T1D managed Y2,Y3 seller in 50-500K / All tenure sellers

Proven Effectiveness

  • 15.5% tier-up rate (+1,318bps vs. unmanaged similar cohort) via rule-based seller selection (GMS Size/YoY, FBA BA YoY, Inventory Health, merchandising/ads activity).
  • Initial response to tier-down risks (5.7% of total T1 sellers): Monitoring deceleration signals (FBA BA/merchandising/ads decline) and proactive intervention with tailored solutions (launched May).
